Making plans

Talking about holidays

This section provides you with the key language needed to talk about leisure and entertainment.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

你最近忙吗？Nǐ zuìjìn máng ma?Have you been busy recently?
你这周末打算做什么？Nǐ zhè zhōumò dǎsuàn zuò shénme?What do you plan to do this weekend?
我打算去看电影。Wǒ dǎsuàn qù kàn diànyǐng.I plan to go to cinema.
你有兴趣吗？Nǐ yǒu xìngqu ma?Are you interested?
什么时候？Shénme shíhòu?What time?
没问题。Méi wèntí.No problem.
晚会很有意思。Wǎnhuì hěn yǒu yìsi.The evening party was very interesting.
玩得很开心。Wǎn de hěn kāixīn.Had a good time.
太可惜了。Tài kěxī le.It was a pity.
你也去吧。Nǐ yě qù ba.You should also go.
但愿不会太忙。Dànyuàn bù huì tài máng.I wish I wasn't so busy.

小陈 ：小王，昨天学院的万圣节晚会你去了吗？
小王 ：没有。你去了吗？
小陈 ：我去了。晚会很有意思，大家都玩得很开心。
小王 ：哎，我昨天有课，太可惜了。
小陈 ：没关系，下周小明过生日，我们都会去他家玩，你也去吧。
小王 ：好啊，但愿我下周不会太忙。
小陈 ：你这个圣诞节打算怎么过？
小王 ：我会去瑞士，和一些同学一起去滑雪。你呢？
小陈 ：我打算回国看看父母，我已经两年没有回家了。
  
Xiǎo Chén:Xiǎo Wáng, zuótiān xuéyuàn de wànshèngjié wǎnhuì nǐ qù le ma?
Xiǎo Wáng: Méiyǒu. Nǐ qù le ma?
Xiǎo Chén:Wǒ qù le. Wǎnhuì hěn yǒu yìsi, dàjiā dōu wán de hěn kāixīn.
Xiǎo Wáng:Ai, wǒ zuótiān yǒu kè, tài kěxī le.
Xiǎo Chén:Méi guānxi, xià zhōu Xiǎo Míng guò shēngrì, wǒmen dōu huì qù tā jiā wán, nǐ yě qù ba.
Xiǎo Wáng:Hǎo a, dànyuàn wǒ xiàzhōu bù huì tài máng.
Xiǎo Chén:Nǐ zhè gè shèngdànjié dǎsuàn zěnme guò?
Xiǎo Wáng:Wǒ huì qù Ruìshì, hé yīxiē tóngxué yīqǐ qù huáxuě. Nǐ ne?
Xiǎo Chén:Wǒ dǎsuàn huíguó kànkan fùmǔ, wǒ yǐjīng liǎngnián méiyǒu huíjiā le.

Chen:Wang, did you go to the college Halloween party last night?
Wang: No. Did you?
Chen:Yes. It was really interesting, everybody had a good time.
Wang:Oh, I had lesson yesterday, it was really a pity.
Chen:No worries, Ming is going to have his birthday next week and we will all go to his place, you could come as well.
Wang:OK, I just hope I won't be too busy next week.
Chen:How do you plan to spend this Christmas?
Wang:I am going to Switzerland, I am going skiing there with some classmates. What about you?
Chen:I plan to go back to my country to visit my parents. I haven't been back for two years.
