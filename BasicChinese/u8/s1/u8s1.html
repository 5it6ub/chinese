Making plans

Talking about holidays

This section provides you with the key language needed to talk about leisure and entertainment.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

你最近忙吗？Nǐ zuìjìn máng ma?Are you busy recently?
你这周末打算做什么？Nǐ zhè zhōumò dǎsuàn zuò shénme?What do you plan to do this weekend?
我打算去看电影。Wǒ dǎsuàn qù kàn diànyǐng.I plan to go to cinema.
你有兴趣吗？Nǐ yǒu xìngqu ma?Are you interested?
什么时候？Shénme shíhòu?What time?
没问题。Méi wèntí.No problem.
晚会很有意思。Wǎnhuì hěn yǒu yìsi.The evening-party was very interesting.
玩得很开心。Wǎn de hěn kāixīn.Had a good time.
太可惜了。Tài kěxī le.It was a pity.
你也去吧。Nǐ yě qù ba.You should also go.
但愿不会太忙。Dànyuàn bù huì tài máng.I wish I wasn't so busy.

小陈 ：嗨，好久不见！
小王 ：是啊，好久不见了。你最近忙吗？
小陈 ：最近忙极了。我刚完成一个项目。你这周末打算做什么？
小王 ：我还没有计划。你呢？
小陈 ：我打算叫几个朋友一起去看电影，007。你有兴趣吗？
小王 ：好啊，什么时候？
小陈 ：周六晚上，我们先一起吃晚饭吧，然后一起去电影院。
小王 ：好，没问题。
  
Xiǎo Chén: Hài, hǎo jiǔ bù jiàn!
Xiǎo Wáng: Shì a, hǎo jiǔ bù jiàn le. Nǐ zuìjìn máng ma?
Xiǎo Chén:Zuìjìn máng jí le. Wǒ gāng wánchéng yī gè xiàngmù. Nǐ zhè zhōumò dǎsuàn zuò shénme?
Xiǎo Wáng:Wǒ hái méiyǒu jìhuà. Nǐ ne?
Xiǎo Chén:Wǒ dǎsuàn jiào jǐ gè péngyǒu yīqǐ qù kàn diànyǐng, línglíngqī. Nǐ yǒu xìngqu ma?
Xiǎo Wáng:Hǎo a, shénme shíhòu?
Xiǎo Chén:Zhōu liù wǎnshàng, wǒmen xiān yīqǐ chī wǎnfàn ba, ránhòu yīqǐ qù diànyǐngyuàn.
Xiǎo Wáng:Hǎo, méiwèntí.

Chen:Hi, long time no see!
Wang: Yes, long time no see. Have you been busy recently?
Chen:I have been extremely busy recently. I've just finished a project. What do you plan to do this weekend?
Wang:I haven't got any plans yet. What about you?
Chen:I plan to ask a few friends to go to the cinema together, 007. Are you interested?
Wang:Yes, what time?
Chen:Saturday evening, shall we have dinner together first and then go to the cinema together?
Wang:Sure, no problem.
