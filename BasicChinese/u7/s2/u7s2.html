Talking about hobbies

This section provides you with the key language needed to talk about hobbies and leisure.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

你喜欢做什么？Nǐ xǐhuān zuò shénme?What do you like doing?
我喜欢打篮球。Wǒ xǐhuān dǎ lánqiú.I like playing basketball.
你喜欢古典音乐还是流行音乐？Nǐ xǐhuān gǔdiǎn yīnyuè háishì liúxíng yīnyuè?Do you like classical music or pop music?
我更喜欢古典音乐。Wǒ gèng xǐhuān gǔdiǎn yīnyuè.I prefer classical music.
太棒了。Tài bàng le.That's great.
你有什么兴趣爱好？Nǐ yǒu shénme xìngqù àihào?What hobbies have you got?
我擅长乒乓球。Wǒ shàncháng pīngpāngqiú.I am good at table-tennis.
我只会下中国象棋。Wǒ zhǐ huì xià Zhōngguó xiàngqí.I can only play Chinese chess.
有机会的话yǒu jīhuì de huàif there is a chance
你觉得怎么样？Nǐ júede zěnmeyàng?What do you think?
真不错。Zhēn bùcuò.Quite good.

小王 ：你有些什么兴趣爱好？
小陈 ：我喜欢体育运动，我最喜欢的是网球，游泳，和国际象棋。你呢？
小王 ：我比较擅长乒乓球。我也喜欢下棋，可是我不会下国际象棋，我只会下中国象棋。
小陈 ：有机会的话，你教我中国象棋，我教你国际象棋，怎么样？
小王 ：好啊。对了，我最近在学太极拳，可有意思了。
小陈 ：是嘛。我听说太极拳很难打，你觉得怎么样？
小王 ：我觉得还行，不是太难。太极拳很慢，正好让我放松身心。
小陈 ：真不错。有机会我也试试。
  
Xiǎo Wáng: Nǐ yǒu xiē shénme xìngqù àihào?
Xiǎo Chén: Wǒ xǐhuān tǐyù yùndòng, wǒ zuì xǐhuān de shì wǎngqiú, yóuyǒng, hé guójì xiàngqí. Nǐ ne?
Xiǎo Wáng:Wǒ bǐjiào shàncháng pīngpāngqiú. Wǒ yě xǐhuān xiàqí, kěshì wǒ bù huì xià guójì xiàngqí, wǒ zhǐ huì xià zhōngguó xiàngqí.
Xiǎo Chén:Yǒu jīhuì de huà, nǐ jiāo wǒ zhōngguó xiàngqí, wǒ jiāo nǐ guójì xiàngqí, zěnmeyàng?
Xiǎo Wáng:Hǎo a. Duì le, wǒ zuìjìn zài xué tàijíquán, kě yǒu yìsi le.
Xiǎo Chén:Shì ma. Wǒ tīngshuō tàijíquán hěn nán dǎ, nǐ juéde zěnmeyàng?
Xiǎo Wáng:Wǒ juéde hái xíng, bù shì tài nán. Tàijíquán hěn màn, zhènghǎo ràng wǒ fàngsōng shēnxīn.
Xiǎo Chén:Zhēn bùcuò. Yǒu jīhuì wǒ yě shìshi.

Wang: What hobbies do you have?
Chen: I like sports, my favourites are tennis, swimming, and chess. And you?
Wang:I am quite good at pingpong. I also like chess, but I cannot play international chess, I can only play Chinese chess.
Chen:If there is a chance, you teach me Chinese chess, I teach you international chess, how about that?
Wang:Sure. Oh, yes, I am learning shadow-boxing recently, which is really interesting.
Chen:Really? I hear that shadow-boxing is really difficult, what do you think?
Wang:I find it ok, not too difficult. Shadow-boxing is very slow, it can let me relax my body and my mind.
Chen:That's really good. I would also have a try if there is a chance.
