Talking about hobbies

This section provides you with the key language needed to talk about hobbies and leisure.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

你喜欢做什么？Nǐ xǐhuān zuò shénme?What do you like doing?
我喜欢打篮球。Wǒ xǐhuān dǎ lánqiú.I like playing basketball.
你喜欢古典音乐还是流行音乐？Nǐ xǐhuān gǔdiǎn yīnyuè háishì liúxíng yīnyuè?Do you like classical music or pop music?
我更喜欢古典音乐。Wǒ gèng xǐhuān gǔdiǎn yīnyuè.I prefer classical music.
太棒了。Tài bàng le.That's great.
你有什么兴趣爱好？Nǐ yǒu shénme xìngqù àihào?What hobbies have you got?
我擅长乒乓球。Wǒ shàncháng pīngpāngqiú.I am good at table-tennis.
我只会下中国象棋。Wǒ zhǐ huì xià Zhōngguó xiàngqí.I can only play Chinese chess.
有机会的话yǒu jīhuì de huàif there is a chance
你觉得怎么样？Nǐ júede zěnmeyàng?What do you think?
真不错。Zhēn bùcuò.Quite good.

小王 ：你平时喜欢做些什么？
小陈 ：我喜欢打打篮球，踢踢足球。你呢？
小王 ：我的爱好可多了。我喜欢听音乐，看电影，画画，还喜欢逛街。
小陈 ：你喜欢古典音乐还是流行音乐？
小王 ：我更喜欢古典音乐。你呢？
小陈 ：我最喜欢的是爵士乐。你喜欢书法吗？
小王 ：当然，我学书法学了五年了。
小陈 ：太棒了。我很喜欢书法，可我只会欣赏，自己不会写。
  
Xiǎo Wáng: Nǐ píngshí xǐhuān zuò xiē shénme?
Xiǎo Chén: Wǒ xǐhuān dǎdǎ lánqiú, tītī zúqiú. Nǐ ne?
Xiǎo Wáng:Wǒ de àihào kě duō le. Wǒ xǐhuān tīng yīnyuè, kàn diànyǐng, huàhuà, hái xǐhuān guàngjiē.
Xiǎo Chén:Nǐ xǐhuān gǔdiǎn yīnyuè háishì liúxíng yīnyuè?
Xiǎo Wáng:Wǒ gèng xǐhuān gǔdiǎn yīnyuè. Nǐ ne?
Xiǎo Chén:Wǒ zuì xǐhuān de shì juéshì yuè. Nǐ xǐhuān shūfǎ ma?
Xiǎo Wáng:Dāngrán, wǒ xué shūfǎ xué le wǔ nián le.
Xiǎo Chén:Tài bàng le. Wǒ hěn xǐhuān shūfǎ, kě wǒ zhǐ huì xīnshǎng, zìjǐ bù huì xiě.

Wang: What do you like doing at your spare time?
Chen: I like playing basketball and football. And you?
Wang:I have a lot of hobbies. I like listening to music, watching movies, painting, and also shopping.
Chen:Do you like classical music or pop music?
Wang:I prefer classical music. And you?
Chen:My favourite is Jazz. Do you like calligraphy?
Wang:Sure, I have learnt calligraphy for five years.
Chen:That's great. I like calligraphy very much, but I can only appreciate, I cannot do it myself.
