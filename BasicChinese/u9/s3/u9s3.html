This section provides you with the key language needed for shopping.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

苹果多少钱一斤？Píngguǒ duōshǎo qián yī jīn?How much are apples per jin?
给我来两斤。Gěi wǒ lái liǎng jīn.Give me two jins.
总共多少钱？Zǒnggòng duōshǎo qián?How much in all?
我更喜欢橘子味的。Wǒ gèng xǐhuān júzi wèi de.I prefer orange-flavour.
有点儿贵呀。Yǒudiǎnr guì ya.A bit expensive.
那算了。Nà suàn le.Then let’s forget about it.
要不买一盒吧?Yàobù mǎi yī hé ba?Shall we take a box of it?
看一下别的。Kàn yīxià biéde.look at something else
挺好的。Tǐng hǎo de.quite good
你需要什么?Nǐ xūyào shénme?What are you looking for?

不错bùcuògood, not bad

顾客一 ：哦,我想起来了。嗯,家里有鸡肉, 我们可以买一点香菇。
顾客二 ：嗯,香菇挺好的,挺有营养的。
顾客一 ：嗯,哦,这儿还有木耳。
顾客二 ：木耳......
顾客一 ：木耳可以炒肉啊,炒鸡蛋。
顾客二 ：嗯,木耳也好,但是木耳就是不好清洗。
顾客一 ：哦,那算了。
顾客二 ：买一袋香菇吧。
顾客一 ：嗯。

Customer 1:Oh, I just remembered. Um, there is chicken at home. We might buy some mushrooms.
Customer 2:Yes, mushrooms are quite good, quite nutritious.
Customer 1:Yes, oh, here are wood-ears as well.
Customer 2:Wood-ears...
Customer 1:Wood-ears can be fried with eggs, meat.
Customer 2:Yes, wood-ears are also good, but wood-ears are difficult to wash.
Customer 1:Oh, then just forget about it.
Customer 2:Let's buy a package of mushrooms.
Customer 1:OK.
