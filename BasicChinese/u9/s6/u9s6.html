Buying a present

This section provides you with the key language needed for shopping.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

苹果多少钱一斤？Píngguǒ duōshǎo qián yī jīn?How much are apples per jin?
给我来两斤。Gěi wǒ lái liǎng jīn.Give me two jins.
总共多少钱？Zǒnggòng duōshǎo qián?How much in all?
我更喜欢橘子味的。Wǒ gèng xǐhuān júzi wèi de.I prefer orange-flavour.
有点儿贵呀。Yǒudiǎnr guì ya.A bit expensive.
那算了。Nà suàn le.Then let’s forget about it.
要不买一盒吧?Yàobù mǎi yī hé ba?Shall we take a box of it?
看一下别的。Kàn yīxià biéde.look at something else
挺好的。Tǐng hǎo de.quite good
你需要什么?Nǐ xūyào shénme?What are you looking for?
我想给我的朋友买一件生日礼物Wǒ xiǎng gěi wǒ de péngyou mǎi yī jiàn shēngri lǐwù.I am looking for a birthday present for my friend.
不错bùcuògood, not bad
您能给打个折吗?Nín néng gěi dǎ ge zhé ma?Can you give me a discount?
你觉得多少钱合适呢?Nǐ juéde duōshǎo qián héshì ne?What price would you consider appropriate?
真漂亮zhēn piàoliangvery beautiful
适合你shìhé nǐIt suits you.
我昨天刚买的wǒ zuótiān gāng mǎi deI only bought it yesterday.
最近在打折zuìjìn zài dǎ zhéjust reduced
我来看一下Wǒ lái kàn yī xiàI am just looking.
我们这儿有wǒmen zhèr yǒuWe have here ...
你需要多大号的?Ní xūyào duō dà hào de?What size do you need?
请问试衣间在哪儿?Qǐngwèn shì yī jiān zài nǎr?Excuse me, where are the changing rooms?
这件太小了Zhè jiàn tài xiǎo leThis one is too small.
发票在包里Fāpiào zài bāo lǐThe receipt is in the bag.
给您卡Gěi nín kǎHere is the card.
欢迎再来Huānyíng zàiláiYou are welcome, please come again.
有大一号的吗yǒu dà yī hào de mado you have it a size bigger?
去老师家做客qù lǎoshi jiā zuò jiāto visit the teacher at home
带礼物去他家dài lǐwù qù tā jiāto take a present to his house
一瓶红酒怎么样yī pīng hōng jiǔ zěnme yàngHow about a bottle of wine?
好主意hǎo zhùyìGood idea
喜欢不喜欢xǐhuan bù xǐhuanDo you like it or not?
或者我可以带一束花huòzhe wǒ kěyǐ dài yī shù huāPerhaps we can take a bunch of flowers

顾客 ：总共多少钱?
收银员 ：嗯,一百元。
顾客 ：给您。
  
Shōuyínyuán :Nín hǎo, huānyíng guānglín.
