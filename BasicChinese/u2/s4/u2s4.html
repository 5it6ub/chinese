This section provides you with the key language needed to talk about origin and languages.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

你是哪国人?Nǐ shì nǎ guó rén?What is your nationality?
我是中国人。Wǒ shì Zhōngguó rén.I am Chinese.
我是新加坡人。Wǒ shì Xīnjiāpō rén.I am Singaporean.
我是法国人。Wǒ shì Fǎguó rén.I am French.
我是英国人。Wǒ shì Yīngguó rén.I am British.
你从哪里来?Nǐ cóng nǎlǐ lái?Where do you come from?
我从德国来。Wǒ cóng Déguó lái.I come from Germany.
我从意大利来。Wǒ cóng Yìdàlì lái.I come from Italy.
我从美国来。Wǒ cóng Měiguó lái.I come from America.
我从爱尔兰来。Wǒ cóng Ài'ěrlán lái.I come from Ireland.
你家在哪里?Nǐ jiā zài nǎlǐ?Where is your home?
我家在上海。Wǒ jiā zài Shànghǎi.My home is at Shanghai.
我家在香港。Wǒ jiā zài Xiānggǎng.My home is at Hongkong.
我家在西安。Wǒ jiā zài Xī'ān.My home is at Xi'an.
我会说英语。Wǒ huì shuō Yīngyǔ.I can speak English.
我正在学习中文。Wǒ zhèngzài xuéxí Zhōngwén.I am learning Chinese.
我的母语是德语。Wǒ de mǔyǔ shì Déyǔ.My mother tongue is German.
你说普通话还是广东话?Nǐ shuō pǔtōnghuà háishì guǎngdōnghuà?Do you speak Mandarin or Cantonese?
哪里，哪里。nǎlǐ, nǎlǐ.Not really.

保罗：小月, 你会说英语吗?
小月:会, 我会说英语, 也会说一点法语和德语。你会说什么欧洲语言?
保罗：我会说英语,法语,西班牙语和意大利语。你会说广东话吗?
小月：我会说普通话, 不会说广东话。

Bǎoluó:Xiǎoyuè, nǐ huì shuō Yīngyǔ ma?
Xiǎoyuè:Huì, wǒ huì shuō Yīngyǔ, yě huì shuō yīdiǎn Fǎyǔ hé Déyǔ. Nǐ huì shuō shénme Ōuzhōu yǔyán?
Bǎoluó:Wǒ huì shuō Yīngyǔ, Fǎyǔ, Xībānyáyǔ hé Yìdàlìyǔ. Nǐ huì shuō Guǎngdōng huà ma?
Xiǎoyuè:Wǒ huì shuō Pǔtōnghuà, bù huì shuō Guǎngdōnghuà.

Paul:Xiaoyue, do you speak English?
Xiaoyue:Yes, I can speak English, I also speak a little French and German. What European languages do you speak?
Paul:I speak English, French, Spanish and Italian. Do you speak Cantonese?
Xiaoyue:I speak Mandarin, I don't speak Cantonese.
