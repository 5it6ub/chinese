Ordering Drinks 2

This section provides you with the key language needed in a café.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

欢迎光临Huānyíng guānglínWelcome
几位?Jǐ wèi?How many of you are there?
你们要喝点什么？Nǐmen yào hē diǎn shénme?What do you want to drink?
要加糖吗？Yào jiā táng ma?Do you want to add some sugar?
我不喜欢喝甜的Wǒ bù xīhuān hē tián de.I don’t like drinking something sweet.
请稍等Qǐng shāoděng.Please wait for a second.
AA制AA zhìgo Dutch
让我们庆祝一下Ràng wǒmen qìngzhù yīxià.Let’s celebrate it.
祝贺你考试顺利通过Zhùhè nǐ kǎoshì shùnlì tōngguò.Congratulations on your passing the exam.
干杯Gānbēi.Cheers.

服务员 :欢迎光临! 您几位?
顾客一 :我们两位。
服务员 :你们好! 你们要喝点儿什么?
顾客一 :来点儿茶。你们有什么茶?
服务员 :我们有红茶，绿茶和花茶。
顾客一 :我喝绿茶。
顾客二 :我喝一杯红茶。
服务员 :要加奶吗？
顾客二 :加，加一点点。
服务员 :要加糖吗?
顾客二 :不要，我不喜欢喝甜的。
服务员 :好的。一杯绿茶，一杯红茶。请稍等。
服务员 :这是您的绿茶。
顾客一 :谢谢。
服务员 :这是您的红茶。
顾客二 :谢谢。
服务员 :不客气。
  
Fúwùyuán:Huānyíng guānglín! Nín jǐ wèi?
Gùkè yī:Wǒmen liǎng wèi.
Fúwùyuán:Nǐmen hǎo! Nǐmen yào hē diǎnr shénme?
Gùkè yī:Lái diǎnr chá. Nǐmen yǒu shénme chá?
Fúwùyuán:Wǒmen yǒu hóngchá, lǜchá hé huāchá
Gùkè yī:Wǒ hē lǜchá.
Gùkè èr:Wǒ hē yī bēi hóngchá.
Fúwùyuán:Yào jiā nǎi ma?
Gùkè èr:Jiā, jiā yīdiǎndiǎn.
Fúwùyuán:Yào jiā táng ma?
Gùkè èr:Bù yào, wǒ bù xǐhuān hē tián de.
Fúwùyuán:Hǎo de. Yī bēi lǜchá, yī bēi hóngchá. Qǐng shāoděng.
Fúwùyuán:Zhè shì nín de lǜchá.
Gùkè yī:Xièxie.
Fúwùyuán:Zhè shì nín de hóngchá.
Gùkè èr:Xièxie.
Fúwùyuán:Bùkèqi.

Waitress:Welcome! How many of you?
Customer 1:There are two of us.
Waitress:Hello! What do you want to drink?
Customer 1:Some tea please. What tea have you got?
Waitress:We have black tea, green tea and jasmine tea
Customer 1:I'll have green tea
Customer 2:I'll have a cup of black tea.
Waitress:With milk?
Customer 2:Yes, a little bit
Waitress:With sugar?
Customer 2:No, I don't like drinking something sweet
Waitress:OK. A cup of green tea, a cup of black tea. Please wait a moment.
Waitress:This is your green tea.
Customer 1:Thanks.
Waitress:This is your black tea.
Customer 2:Thanks.
Waitress:You are welcome.
