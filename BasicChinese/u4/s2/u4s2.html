Ordering Drinks 2

This section provides you with the key language needed in a café.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

服务员 :你们喝点儿什么?
女客人 :我要一杯柠檬茶。你呢?
男客人 :我要一杯咖啡吧。
服务员 :小姐, 您的柠檬茶要加冰吗?
女客人 :不加冰。我不喜欢喝太冷的饮料。
服务员 :先生,您的咖啡要加奶和糖吗?
男客人 :请给我加一点点牛奶,不要放糖。
  
Fúwùyuán:Nǐmen hē diǎnr shénme?
Nǚ kèrén:Wǒ yào yī bēi níngméng chá. Nǐ ne?
Nán kèrén:Wǒ yào yī bēi kāfēi ba.
Fúwùyuán:Xiǎojiě, nín de níngméng chá yào jiā bīng ma?
Nǚ kèrén:Bù jiā bīng. Wǒ bù xǐhuān hē tài lěng de yǐnliào.
Fúwùyuán:Xiānsheng, nín de kāfēi yào jiā nǎi hé táng ma?
Nán kèrén:Qǐng gěi wǒ jiā yīdiǎndiǎn niúnǎi, bù yào fàng táng.

Waitress:What would you like to drink?
Female customer:I would like a cup of lemon tea. How about you?
Male customer:I would like a cup of coffee.
Waitress:Miss, would you like ice in your lemon tea?
Female customer:No I don't like cold drinks.
Waitress:Sir, Would you like milk or sugar in your coffee?
Male customer:A little bit of milk please and no sugar.
