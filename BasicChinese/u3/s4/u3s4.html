Talking about the family

My Family (extended)

This section provides you with the key language needed to make contact.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

二十四岁èrshísì suìtwenty-four years of age
我是研究生wǒ shì yánjiūshēngI am a research student.
我学数学wǒ xué shùxuéI am studying mathematics.
我来自南京wǒ láizì NánjīngI am from Nanjing.
我的专业是经济wǒ de zhuānyè shì jīngjìMy subject is economics,
他在银行上班tā zài yínháng shàngbānHe works at a bank.
我们有一个儿子wǒmen yǒu yī gè érziWe have a son.
你有兄弟姐妹吗?nǐ yǒu xiōngdìjiěmèi maDo you have brothers and sisters?
她有一个姐姐和一个弟弟tā yǒu yī gè jiějie hé yī gè dìdiShe has an older sister and a younger brother.
我是独生子wǒ shì dúshēngzǐI am a lone child.
你有叔叔阿姨吗nǐ yǒu shūshu āyí maDo you have uncles and aunts?
你多大了nǐ duō dà leHow old are you?
你几岁了nǐ jǐ suì leHow old are you?
我父亲退休了, 我妈妈还工作wǒ fùqin tùixiū le, wǒ māma hái gōngzuòMy father has retired, my mother still works.
我在一家电脑公司工作wǒ zài yī jiā diànnǎo gōngsī gōngzuò.I work for a computer company.

Amanda: 杨帆，你有兄弟姐妹吗？
苏宁 :没有，我是家里的独生子。
Amanda: 你有叔叔阿姨吗？
苏宁 :有，我爸爸有两个哥哥，我妈妈有一个妹妹。
Amanda: 你们都住在一起吗？
苏宁 :没有，我爸爸和爷爷奶奶住在杭州，我的叔叔阿姨住在南京。
Amanda: 你属什么？
苏宁 :我属狗。Amanda，你呢？ 你家有几口人？
Amanda: 我家有六口人，有我的爸爸妈妈,两个姐姐,弟弟,还有我。
苏宁 :你弟弟今年多大了
Amanda: 我弟弟今年二十一岁。他还在读书。
苏宁 :他学什么专业？
Amanda: 他学建筑，他以后想当个设计师。
苏宁 :你父母都还在工作吗？
Amanda: 我爸爸还在工作，他在一个贸易公司上班。
苏宁 :你妈妈呢？
Amanda: 我妈妈已经退休了，她以前是化学老师。
苏宁 :你现在在哪里上班？
Amanda: 我在伦敦的一家电脑公司上班，我是个软件工程师。

Amanda: Yáng Fān, nǐ yǒu xiōngdìjiěmèi ma?
Sū Níng:Méiyǒu, wǒ shì jiā lǐ de dúshēngzì.
Amanda: Nǐ yǒu shūshu āyí ma?
Sū Níng:Yǒu, wǒ bàba yǒu liǎng ge gēge, wǒ māma yǒu yī ge mèimei.
Amanda: Nǐmen dōu zhù zài yīqǐ ma?
Sū Níng:Méiyǒu, wǒ bàba hé yéye nǎinai zhù zài Hángzhōu, wǒ de shūshu āyí zhù zài Nánjīng.
Amanda: Nǐ shǔ shénme?
Sū Níng:Wǒ shǔ gǒu. Amanda, nǐ ne? Nǐjiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rén?
Amanda: Wǒ jiā yǒu liù kǒu rén, yǒu wǒde bàbamǎma, liǎng ge jiějie, dìdi, háiyǒu wǒ.
Sū Níng:Nǐ dìdi jīnnián duō dà le?
Amanda: Wǒ dìdi jīnnián èr shí yī suì, tā háizài dú shū.
Sū Níng:Tā xué shénme zhuānyè?
Amanda: Tā xué jiànzhù, tā yǐhòu xiǎng dāng ge shèjìshī.
Sū Níng:Nǐ fùmǔ dōu háizài gōngzuò ma?
Amanda: Wǒ bàba háizài gōngzuò, tā zài yī gè màoyì gōngsī shàngbān.
Sū Níng:Nǐ māma ne?
Amanda: Wǒ māma yǐjīng tuìxiū le, tā yǐqián shì huàxué lǎoshī.
Sū Níng:Nǐ xiànzài zài nǎlǐ shàngbān?
Amanda: Wǒ zài Lúndūn de yī jiā diànnǎo gōngsī shàngbān, wǒ shì ge ruǎnjiàn gōngchéngshī.

Amanda:Fan Yang, have you got siblings?
Fan Yang:No, I am the only son in my family.
Amanda:Do you have uncles and aunts?
Fan Yang:Yes, my dad has two older brothers, and my mum has one younger sister.
Amanda:Do you all live together?
Fan Yang:No, my dad lives with my paternal grandparents in Hangzhou, my uncles and aunts live in Nanjing.
Amanda:What is your animal sign?
Fan Yang:I am a dog. What about you, Amanda? How many people are there in your family?
Amanda:There are six people, my parents, two older sisters, my younger brother, and me.
Fan Yang:How old is your younger brother?
Amanda:He is 21 this year, he still goes to university.
Fan Yang:What subject does he do?
Amanda:He studies architecture, and he wants to be a designer in the future.
Fan Yang:Are your parents still working?
Amanda:My dad is, he works for a trading company.
Fan Yang:What about your mum?
Amanda:My mum has already retired. She used to be a chemistry teacher.
Fan Yang:Where do you work now?
Amanda:I work for a computer company at London, I am a software engineer.
