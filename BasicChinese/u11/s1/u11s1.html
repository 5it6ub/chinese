Booking train tickets

At the Tourist Office

This section provides you with the key language needed to talk about travel.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

去西安的车票qù Xī'ān de chēpiàoticket to go to Xi'an
您要哪个车次Nín yào nǎ gè chēcì?Which train time do you want?
我想早上走Wǒ xiǎng zǎoshàng zǒu.I want to leave in the morning.
稍等shāoděngwait a second
给你查一下gěi nǐ chá yīxiàcheck for you
您要硬卧还是软卧Nín yào yìngwò háishì ruǎnwò?Would you want a hard sleeper or a soft sleeper?
明天来取票míngtiān lái qǔ piàocome tomorrow to collect the ticket
比较好玩bǐjiào hǎowáncomparatively fun
都可以dōu kěyǐall are fine

女 ：您好，请问有去西安的车票吗？
男 ：您要什么时间的？
女 ：四月二十号的。
男 ：您要哪个车次？
女 ：我想早上走。
男 ：稍等，我给你查一下。
男 ：有一趟早上十点二十的火车，第二天中午十二点到西安，您觉得怎么样？
女 ：好的。
男 ：您要硬卧还是软卧？
女 ：我想要软卧，多少钱一张？
男 ：四百元，您可以明天来取票。
女 ：好的，谢谢。那我明天早上来取。
男 ：不客气。

Female: Hello, may I ask if there are tickets to Xi'an?
Male: When do you want to travel?
Female20th April.
Male: Which train do you want?
FemaleI want to go in the morning.
Male: Wait a second. I'll check for you.
Male: There is a train at 10:20am, arriving at Xi'an at 12:00 noon the following day. What do you think?
FemaleOK.
Male: Do you want a hard sleeper or a soft sleeper?
FemaleI want a soft sleeper, how much is a ticket?
Male: 400 yuan. You can come to collect the ticket tomorrow.
FemaleOK, thanks. I'll come to collect the ticket tomorrow morning.
Male: You're welcome.
