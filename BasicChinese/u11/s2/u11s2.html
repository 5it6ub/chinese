Booking train tickets

At the Tourist Office

This section provides you with the key language needed to talk about travel and tourism.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

去西安的车票qù Xī'ān de chēpiàoticket to go to Xi'an
您要哪个车次Nín yào nǎ gè chēcì?Which train time do you want?
我想早上走Wǒ xiǎng zǎoshàng zǒu.I want to leave in the morning.
稍等shāoděngwait a second
给你查一下gěi nǐ chá yīxiàcheck for you
您要硬卧还是软卧Nín yào yìngwò háishì ruǎnwò?Would you want a hard sleeper or a soft sleeper?
明天来取票míngtiān lái qǔ piàocome tomorrow to collect the ticket
比较好玩bǐjiào hǎowáncomparatively fun
都可以dōu kěyǐall are fine

男 ：嗯，您好。我刚来北京。哪里比较好玩？
女 ：故宫、颐和园、天坛，都不错。
男 ：一般去哪里买东西？
女 ：买东西一般去王府井，就在北京饭店旁边。
男 ：嗯，好的，谢谢。嗯，能给我一张北京地图吗？
女 ：好的，这儿有一张。
男 ：怎么去比较方便?
女 ：坐公交车、地铁，都可以，打的也不贵。
  
nán:Ēn, nín hǎo. Wǒ gāng lái Běijīng. Nǎlǐ bǐjiào háowán?
nǚ:Gògōng, Yíhéyuán, Tiāntán, dōu bùcuò.
nán:Yībān qù nǎlǐ mǎi dōngxi?
nǚ:Mǎi dōngxi yībān qù Wángfǔjǐng, jiù zài Běijīng fàndiàn pángbiān.
nán:Ēn, hǎode, xièxie. Ēn, néng gěi wǒ yī zhāng Běijīng dìtú ma?
nǚ: Hǎode, zhèr yǒu yī zhāng.
nán: Zěnme qù bǐjiào fāngbiàn?
nǚ: Zuò gōngjiāochē, dìtiě, dōu kěyǐ, dǎdī yě bù guì.

Male:Um, hello. I've just arrived in Beijing. Where is nice to be?
Female: The Forbidden City, the Summer Palace, the Temple of Heaven, are all good.
Male:Where do people usually go shopping?
Female:People usually go to Wangfujing to do shopping, which is just next to the Beijing Hotel.
Male:OK, good, thanks. Um, can you give me a map of Beijing?
Female:OK, here is one.
Male:How to get there?
Female:You can take a bus, or the underground, both are fine. It is not expensive to go by taxi either.
