This section provides you with the key language needed in a hotel.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

请问您贵姓？Qǐngwèn nín guì xìng?May I ask what your surname is please?
有电视，还有热水Yǒu diànshì, háiyǒu rèshuǐ.There is TV, and hot water as well.
包括早餐吗？Bāokuò zǎocān ma?Is breakfast included?
真抱歉Zhēn bào qiàn.I am really sorry.
我预定了Wǒ yùdìng le.I have booked it.
还可以上网Hái kěyǐshàngwǎng.(You) can also have access to internet.
房间怎么样？Fángjiān zěnmeyàng?How is the room?
能不能看看你的身份证Néngbùnéng kànkan nǐde shēnfènzhèng?May I have a look at your ID card?
这是您的钥匙Zhèshì nínde yàoshiThis is your key.
我想退房Wǒ xiǎng tuìfáng.I want to have my room checked out.
我住345房间Wǒ zhù sān sì wǔ fángjiānI live in Room 345.
我马上找人去修Wǒ mǎshàng zhǎo rén qù xiū.I’ll find someone to fix it right now.
空调坏了Kōngtiáo huài le.The air conditioner is broken.

接待人员 ：您好。
顾客 ：你好。
接待人员 ：请问你有预定吗?
顾客 ：我预定了,我打电话预定的
接待人员 ：好的。先生,请问您贵姓?
顾客 ：我姓王。
接待人员 ：好的, 先生,您的预定是三天吗?
顾客 ：三天。房间怎么样?
接待人员 ：每个房间都有电视、空调,还可以上网。请您填一下登记表好吗?
顾客 ：好的。
接待人员 ：好的,谢谢......先生,能不能看看您的身份证?
顾客 ：这是我的身份证。
接待人员 ：好的......好,谢谢......这是您房间的钥匙,345房间,在三层
顾客 ：谢谢。
接待人员 ：不客气。
  
Jiēdài rényuán :Nín hǎo.
Gùkè :Nǐ hǎo.
Jiēdài rényuán :Qǐngwèn nǐ yǒu yùdìng ma?
Gùkè :Wǒ yùdìng le, wǒ dǎ diànhuà yùdìng de.
Jiēdài rényuán :Hǎode. Xiānsheng, qǐngwèn nín guì xìng?
Gùkè :Wǒ xìng wáng.
Jiēdài rényuán :Hǎo de, xiānsheng, nín de yùdìng shì sān tiān ma?
Gùkè :Sān tiān. Fángjiān zěnmeyàng?
Jiēdài rényuán :Měi gè fángjiān dōu yǒu diànshì, kōngtiáo, hái kěyǐ shàngwǎng. Qǐng nín tián yīxià dēngjìbiǎo hǎo ma?
Gùkè :Hǎo de.
Jiēdài rényuán :Hǎo de, xièxie...xiānsheng, néng bù néng kànkan nín de shēnfēnzhèng
Gùkè :Zhè shì wǒ de shēnfènzhèng
Jiēdài rényuán :Hǎo de...hǎo, xièxie...zhè shì nín fángjiān de yàoshi, sān sì wǔ fángjiān, zài sān céng.
Gùkè :Xièxie.
Jiēdài rényuán :Bùkèqi.

Receptionist:Hello.
Customer:Hello.
Receptionist:May I ask if you have booked or not?
Customer:Yes, I have. I booked over the phone.
Receptionist:Fine. Sir, may I have your surname please?
Customer:My surname is Wang.
Receptionist:Fine. Sir, did you book for three days?
Customer:Three days. How's the room?
Receptionist:Each room has TV, air-conditioning and access to internet. Could you please fill in the registration form?
Customer:OK.
Receptionist:Fine, thanks...Sir, may I take a look at your identity card?
Customer:This is my identity card.
Receptionist:Good... Fine, thanks...This is the key to your room, Room 345, on the second floor.
Customer:Thanks.
Receptionist:You are welcome.
