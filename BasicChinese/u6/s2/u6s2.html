This section provides you with the key language needed in a hotel.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

请问您贵姓？Qǐngwèn nín guì xìng?May I ask what your surname is please?
有电视，还有热水Yǒu diànshì, háiyǒu rèshuǐ.There is TV, and hot water as well.
包括早餐吗？Bāokuò zǎocān ma?Is breakfast included?
真抱歉Zhēn bào qiàn.I am really sorry.
我预定了Wǒ yùdìng le.I have booked it.
还可以上网Hái kěyǐshàngwǎng.(You) can also have access to internet.
房间怎么样？Fángjiān zěnmeyàng?How is the room?
能不能看看你的身份证Néngbùnéng kànkan nǐde shēnfènzhèng?May I have a look at your ID card?
这是您的钥匙Zhèshì nínde yàoshiThis is your key.
我想退房Wǒ xiǎng tuìfáng.I want to have my room checked out.
我住345房间Wǒ zhù sān sì wǔ fángjiānI live in Room 345.
我马上找人去修Wǒ mǎshàng zhǎo rén qù xiū.I’ll find someone to fix it right now.
空调坏了Kōngtiáo huài le.The air conditioner is broken.

接待人员 ：您好,这里是近春园宾馆。
顾客 ：你好,我想订个房间
接待人员 ：请问您哪天到,要住几天?
顾客 ：我这个月16号到,19号离开。
接待人员 ：好的,请问您订单人间还是双人间?
顾客 ：房间怎么收费?
接待人员 ：单人间每天400元, 双人间每天600元。
顾客 ：包括早餐吗?
接待人员 ：是的, 还可以上宽带网
顾客 ：请问有空调吗
接待人员 :有。每个房间都有电视、空调, 还有热水。
顾客 :好, 我想订个单人间
接待人员 :好的。请问您贵姓?
顾客 :我姓王
接待人员 :好的, 王先生,已经给您订了一个单人间,从16号到19号。
顾客 :谢谢你。再见。
接待人员 :再见。
  
Jiēdài rényuán :Nínhǎo, zhèlǐ shì Jìnchūnyuán bīnguǎn.
Gùkè :Nǐhǎo, wǒ xiǎng dìng gè fángjiān
Jiēdài rényuán :Qǐngwèn nín nǎ tiān dào, yào zhù jǐ tiān?
Gùkè :Wǒ zhè gè yuè shíliù hào dào, shíjiǔ hào líkāi.
Jiēdài rényuán :Hǎode, qǐngwèn nín dìng dānrénjiān háishì shuāngrénjiān?
Gùkè :Fángjiān zěnme shōufèi?
Jiēdài rényuán :Dānrénjiān měitiān sìbǎi yuán, shuāngrénjiān měitiān liùbǎi yuán.
Gùkè :Bāokuò zǎocān ma?
Jiēdài rényuán :Shìde, hái kěyǐ shàng kuāndàiwǎng.
Gùkè :Qǐngwèn yǒu kōngtiáo ma?
Jiēdài rényuán :Yǒu. Měi gè fángjiān dōuyǒu diànshì, kōngtiáo, háiyǒu rèshuǐ.
Gùkè :Hǎo, wǒ xiǎng dìng gè dānrénjiā
Jiēdài rényuán :Hǎode. Qǐngwèn nín guì xìng?
Gùkè :Wǒ xìng wáng.
Jiēdài rényuán :Hǎode, wáng xiānsheng, yǐjīng gěi nín dìng le yī gè dānrénjiān, cóng shíliù hào dào shíjiǔ hào
Gùkè :Xièxie nǐ. Zàijiàn.
Jiēdài rényuán :Zàijiàn.

Receptionist:Hello, Jinchunyuan Hotel.
Customer:Hello, I would like to book a room.
Receptionist:May I ask on which dates you will arrive and leave?
Customer:I will arrive on the 16th of this month and leave on the 19th.
Receptionist:OK, may I ask would you like to book a single room or a double room?
Customer:How do you charge?
Receptionist:Single rooms cost 400 yuan per day and double rooms cost 600 yuan per day.
Customer:Is breakfast included?
Receptionist:Yes, and also broadband.
Customer:May I ask if there is air-conditioning or not?
Receptionist:Yes. Each room has television, air-conditioning and hot water.
Customer:Good, I want to book a single room.
Receptionist:Sure, may I have your surname please?
Customer:My surname is Wang.
Receptionist:OK, Mr Wang, I have already booked a single room for you, from the 16th to the 19th.
Customer:Thank you. Goodbye.
Receptionist:Goodbye.
