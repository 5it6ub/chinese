This section provides you with the key language needed in a hotel.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

请问您贵姓？Qǐngwèn nín guì xìng?May I ask what your surname is please?
有电视，还有热水Yǒu diànshì, háiyǒu rèshuǐ.There is TV, and hot water as well.
包括早餐吗？Bāokuò zǎocān ma?Is breakfast included?
真抱歉Zhēn bào qiàn.I am really sorry.
我预定了Wǒ yùdìng le.I have booked it.
还可以上网Hái kěyǐshàngwǎng.(You) can also have access to internet.
房间怎么样？Fángjiān zěnmeyàng?How is the room?
能不能看看你的身份证Néngbùnéng kànkan nǐde shēnfènzhèng?May I have a look at your ID card?
这是您的钥匙Zhèshì nínde yàoshiThis is your key.
我想退房Wǒ xiǎng tuìfáng.I want to have my room checked out.
我住345房间Wǒ zhù sān sì wǔ fángjiānI live in Room 345.
我马上找人去修Wǒ mǎshàng zhǎo rén qù xiū.I’ll find someone to fix it right now.
空调坏了Kōngtiáo huài le.The air conditioner is broken.

顾客 ：你好,我想退房。
接待人员 ：请问您住几号房间?
顾客 :345房间
接待人员 ：好的,三天,一共1200块钱。
顾客 ：请问我可以用信用卡吗?
接待人员 ：可以......好的......好的,这是您的发票。
顾客 ：谢谢,再见。
接待人员 ：再见。
  
Gùkè :Nǐ hǎo, wǒ xiǎng tuìfáng.
Jiēdài rényuán :Qǐngwèn nín zhù jǐ hào fángjiān?
Gùkè :Sān sì wǔ fángjiān.
Jiēdài rényuán :Hǎode, sān tiān, yīgòng yī qiān èr bǎi kuài qián.
Gùkè :Qǐngwèn wǒ kěyǐ yòng xìnyòngkǎ ma?
Jiēdài rényuán :Kěyǐ...hǎode...hǎode, zhèshì nín de fāpiào.
Gùkè :Xièxie, zàijiàn
Jiēdài rényuán :Zàijiàn.

Guest:Hello, I want to check out.
Receptionist:May I ask what your room number is please?
Guest:Room 345.
Receptionist:Fine, three days, altogether 1200 yuan.
Guest:May I use my credit card please?
Receptionist:Yes...fine...fine, this is your receipt.
Guest:Thanks. Goodbye.
Receptionist:Goodbye.
