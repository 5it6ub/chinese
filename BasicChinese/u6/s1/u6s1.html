This section provides you with the key language needed in a hotel.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

请问您贵姓？Qǐngwèn nín guì xìng?May I ask what your surname is please?
有电视，还有热水Yǒu diànshì, háiyǒu rèshuǐ.There is TV, and hot water as well.
包括早餐吗？Bāokuò zǎocān ma?Is breakfast included?
真抱歉Zhēn bào qiàn.I am really sorry.
我预定了Wǒ yùdìng le.I have booked it.
还可以上网Hái kěyǐshàngwǎng.(You) can also have access to internet.
房间怎么样？Fángjiān zěnmeyàng?How is the room?
能不能看看你的身份证Néngbùnéng kànkan nǐde shēnfènzhèng?May I have a look at your ID card?
这是您的钥匙Zhèshì nínde yàoshiThis is your key.
我想退房Wǒ xiǎng tuìfáng.I want to have my room checked out.
我住345房间Wǒ zhù sān sì wǔ fángjiānI live in Room 345.
我马上找人去修Wǒ mǎshàng zhǎo rén qù xiū.I’ll find someone to fix it right now.
空调坏了Kōngtiáo huài le.The air conditioner is broken.

接待人员 ：您好。
顾客 ：你好。我想要一个房间。
接待人员 ：请问您要单人间还是双人间？
顾客 ：一个单人间。请问房间多少钱？
接待人员 ：四百块钱。请问您住几天？
顾客 :三天。
接待人员 ：好的，请您填一下登记表。
顾客 :好的。
接待人员 ：好，这是您房间的钥匙，345房间。
顾客 ：请问早饭几点
接待人员 ：早饭从七点到九点半。
顾客 ：谢谢，再见。
接待人员 ：再见。
  
Jiēdài rényuán :Nín hǎo.
Gùkè :Nǐ hǎo. Wǒ xiǎng yào yī gè fángjiān.
Jiēdài rényuán :Qǐngwèn nín yào dānrénjiān háishì shuāngrénjiān?
Gùkè :Yī gè dānrénjiān. Qǐngwèn fángjiān duōshǎo qián?
Jiēdài rényuán :Sì bǎi kuài qián. Qǐngwèn nín zhù jǐ tiān?
Gùkè :Sān tiān.
Jiēdài rényuán :Hǎode, qǐng nín tián yīxià dēngjìbiǎo.
Gùkè :Hǎode.
Jiēdài rényuán :Hǎo. Zhèshì nín fángjiān de yàoshi, sān sì wǔ fángjian.
Gùkè :Qǐngwèn zǎofàn jǐ diǎn?
Jiēdài rényuán :Zǎofàn cóng qī diǎn dào jiǔ diǎn bàn.
Gùkè :Xièxie, zàijiàn.
Jiēdài rényuán :Zàijiàn.

Receptionist:Hello.
Customer:Hello. I want to have a room.
Receptionist:Do you want a single room or a double room?
Customer:A single room. How much is the room?
Receptionist:400 yuan. How many days are you going to stay?
Customer:Three days.
Receptionist:OK, please fill in the registration form.
Customer:OK.
Receptionist:Fine, this is the key to your room. Room 345.
Customer:May I ask what time is the breakfast?
Receptionist:The breakfast is from 7 to 9:30.
Customer:Thanks. Goodbye.
Receptionist:Goodbye.
