男 ：在中国，人们最重视的是什么节日？
女 ：应该是春节吧。春节是中国人的传统节日，是汉族人民最重要的节日。
男 ：春节是在每年的一月一日吗？
女 ：没错，不过是阴历的一月一日，通常是在阳历的二月初。
男 ：哦，我明白了。中国的传统节日还有哪些？
女 ：还有元宵节，清明节，端午节，七夕节，中秋节等等。
男 ：中秋节在什么时候？
女 ：顾名思义，中秋节在秋天，是农历的八月十五。
男 ：人们在中秋节做什么？
女 ：因为中秋节的月亮是一年中最圆的，所以人们通常会赏月，吃月饼。中秋节也是家人团圆的日子。
男 ：其他还有什么节日吗？
女 ：有，中国人还过妇女节，儿童节，教师节等等呢。
男 ：中国人过不过西方的节日？
女 ：过，中国的年轻人都很喜欢过西方的圣诞节，情人节，万圣节等等。
男 ：那中国人不是一直在过节吗？
女 ：没错。
  
Nán: Zài Zhōngguó, rénmen zuì zhòngshì de shì shénme jiérì?
Nǚ:Yīnggāi shì chūnjié ba. Chūnjié shì Zhōngguó rén de chuántǒng jiérì, shì hànzú rénmín zuì zhòngyào de jièrì.
Nán:Chūnjié shì zài měi nián de yī yuè yī rì ma?
Nǚ:Méicuò, bùguò shì yīnlì de yī yuè yī rì, tōngcháng shì zài yánglì de èr yuè chū.
Nán:Ò, wǒ míngbai le. Zhōngguó de chuántǒng jiérì háiyǒu nǎxiē?
Nǚ:Háiyǒu Yuánxiāo jié, Qīngmíng jié, Duānwǔ jié, Qīxī jié, Zhōngqiū jié děngděng.
Nán:Zhōngqiū jié zài shénme shíhou?
Nǚ:Gùmíngsīyì, Zhōngqiū jié zài qiūtiān, shì nónglì de bā yuè shí wǔ.
Nán:Rénmen zài Zhōngqiū jié zuò shénme?
Nǚ:Yīnwèi Zhōngqiū jié de yuèliang shì yī nián zhōng zuì yuán de, suǒyǐ rénmen tōngcháng huì shǎng yuè, chī yuèbǐng. Zhōngqiū jié yě shì jiārén tuányuán de rìzi.
Nán:Qítā háiyǒu shénme jiérì ma?
Nǚ:Yǒu, Zhōngguó rén hái guò Fùnǚ jié, Értóng jié, Jiàoshī jié děngděng ne.
Nán:Zhōngguó rén guò bù guò xīfāng de jiérì?
Nǚ:Guò, Zhōngguó de niánqīngrén dōu hěn xǐhuān guò xīfāng de Shèngdàn jié, Qíngrén jié, Wànshèng jié děngděng.
Nán:Nà Zhōngguó rén bùshì yīzhí zài guòjié ma?
Nǚ:Méicuò.

Male:In China, which festival do people value most?
Female: It should be the Spring Festival. The Spring Festival is the traditional festival of the Chinese people, and the most important festival of the Han ethnic people.
Male:Is the Spring Festival on 1st January every year?
Female:Yes, but it is by the lunar calendar, usually at the beginning of February by the solar calendar.
Male:Oh, I see. What other traditional Chinese festivals are there?
Female:There are Lantern Festival, Tomb-sweeping festival, Dragon Boat Festival, Double Seventh Festival, Mid-Autumn Festival, etc.
Male:When is the Mid-Autumn Festival?
Female:You can tell from itself that it is in autumn, the 15th August by the lunar calendar.
Male:What do people do on that day?
Female:As the moon on Mid-Autumn day is the roundest of a year, therefore people usually look at the moon and eat moon-cakes. It is also a family get-together day.
Male:Are there any other festivals?
Female:Yes, Chinese people also celebrate Women’s Day, Children’s Day, Teachers’ Day, etc.
Male:Do Chinese people celebrate western festivals?
Female:Yes, young people in China like to celebrate Christmas, Valentine’s Day, Halloween, etc.
Male:Then Chinese people are always celebrating festivals, aren’t you?
Female:True.
