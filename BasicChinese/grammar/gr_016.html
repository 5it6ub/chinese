Chinese Grammar - 地 (de)

Index
地 is used to combine verbs and adverbs.

她高兴地唱着。 tā gāoxìng de chàng zhe She is singing happily.


Single syllable adverbs can be used without 地.

您慢走。 nín màn zǒu (You) Please walk slowly.
