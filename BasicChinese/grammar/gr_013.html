Chinese Grammar - Verbal measure words

Index
Verbal measure words indicate how often an action is carried out.

次 (cì) is used to say how many times something happened.

遍 (biàn)is used to emphasise that an activity was carried out for a number of times completely.

趟 (tàng)is used only with verbs of movement like
去 (qù, to go), 走 (zǒu, to walk), 来 (lái, to come), 飞 (fēi, to fly), 跑 (pǎo, to run) etc.


次: 我到北京来过三次。 wǒ dào běijīng lái guò sān cì I have been to Beijing three times.
趟: 她飞了一趟伦敦。 tā fēi le yī tàng lúndūn She has flown to London once.
遍: 他这本小说看了十遍。 tā zhè běn xiǎoshuō kàn le shí biàn He has read this novel (completely) ten times.
