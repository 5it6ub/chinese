接线员 :您好，和平饭店。
顾客 :您好，我想订一桌饭。
接线员 :好的，请问您订哪天的？
顾客 :这个星期六，25号晚上
接线员 :请问一共几位？
顾客 :一共八个人，我们想要一个包间
接线员 :好的，25号晚上，八个人，包间。请问您贵姓？
顾客 :我姓王
接线员 :王先生，你们估计几点到饭店？
顾客 :我们差不多七点到吧。
接线员 :好，我会帮你们把包间保留到七点半，请在七点半前到饭店好吗
顾客 :好的，谢谢。
接线员 :谢谢您，王先生，再见。
  
Jiēxiànyuán :Nín hǎo, Hépíng fàndiàn.
Gùkè :Nín hǎo, wǒ xiǎng dìng yī zhuō fàn.
Jiēxiànyuán :Hǎo de, qǐngwèn nín dìng nǎ tiān de?
Gùkè :Zhè gè xīngqī liù, èr shí wǔ hào wǎnshàng.
Jiēxiànyuán :Qǐngwèn yīgòng jǐ wèi?
Gùkè :Yīgòng bā gè rén, wǒmen xiǎng yào yī gè bāojiān.
Jiēxiànyuán :Hǎo de, èr shí wǔ hào wǎnshàng, bā gè rén, bāojiān. Qǐngwèn nín guì xìng?
Gùkè :Wǒ xìng Wáng
Jiēxiànyuán :Wáng xiānsheng, nǐmen gūjì jǐ diǎn dào fàndiàn?
Gùkè :Wǒmen chàbuduō qī diǎn dào ba.
Jiēxiànyuán :Hǎo, wǒ huì bāng nǐmen bǎ bāojiān bǎoliú dào qī diǎn bàn, qǐng zài qī diǎn bàn qián dào fàndiàn hǎo ma?
Gùkè :Hǎo de, xièxie.
Jiēxiànyuán :Xièxie nín, Wáng xiānsheng, zàijiàn.

Telephone operator:Hello, Peace Hotel.
Customer:Hello, I want to book a table.
Telephone operator:Sure, which day please?
Customer:This Saturday, in the evening of 25th.
Telephone operator:How many are there please?
Customer:Altogether eight. We want a separate room.
Telephone operator:OK, in the evening of 25th, eight, compartment. Your surname please?
Customer:My surname is Wang.
Telephone operator:Mr Wang, what time are you likely to be arriving please?
Customer:Around seven.
Telephone operator:OK, I will reserve the compartment for you until seven thirty. Please arrive before seven thirty, all right?
Customer:OK, thanks.
Telephone operator:Thank you, Mr Wang, goodbye.
