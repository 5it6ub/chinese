顾客 :服务员，请结账
服务员 :李先生，这是找您的50元
顾客 :好的， 谢谢。这账单好像不对。
服务员 :怎么了？
顾客 :我们点的是一杯红酒，不是一瓶红酒
服务员 :非常抱歉，应该是150元
顾客 :能用信用卡吗？
服务员 :对不起，我们只收现金
顾客 :没关系。这是200元。
服务员 :谢谢。欢迎下次再来，请慢走。
  
Gùkè :Fúwùyuán, qǐng jiézhàng.
Fúwùyuán :Lǐ xiānsheng, zhè shì zhǎo nín de wǔ shí yuán.
Gùkè :Hǎo de, xièxie. Zhè zhàngdān hǎoxiàng bùduì.
Fúwùyuán :Zěnmele?
Gùkè :Wǒmen diǎn de shì yī bēi hóngjiǔ, bùshì yī píng hóngjiǔ.
Fúwùyuán :Fēicháng bàoqiàn, yīnggāi shì yī bǎi wǔ shí yuán.
Gùkè :Néng yòng xìnyòngkǎ ma?
Fúwùyuán :Duìbùqǐ, wǒmen zhǐ shōu xiànjīn.
Gùkè :Méiguānxi. Zhè shì èr bǎi yuán.
Fúwùyuán :Xièxie. Huānyíng xià cì zài lái, qǐng mànzǒu.

Customer:Waitress, the bill please.
Waitress:Here is your 50 yuan change, Mr Li
Customer:Good, thanks. The bill does not seem to be correct.
Waitress:What’s wrong?
Customer:We only ordered a glass of red wine, not a bottle of it.
Waitress:I am very sorry about it. It should be 150 yuan.
Customer:Can I use a credit card?
Waitress:I’m sorry, we only accept cash.
Customer:It’s alright. Here is 200 yuan.
Waitress:Thanks. Please come again and good-bye.
