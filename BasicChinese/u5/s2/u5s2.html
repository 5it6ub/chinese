服务员 :欢迎光临！请问有没有预定？
顾客 :有预定。
服务员 :您贵姓？
顾客 :姓李，叫李明。
服务员 :李先生，您的包间是牡丹厅。请跟我来。
服务员 :这是菜单。请问喝点儿什么？
顾客 :我喝一瓶青岛啤酒。
顾客 :我要一杯红葡萄酒
顾客 :我要一壶绿茶。
顾客 :这里什么菜做得好？
服务员 :辣子鸡丁和麻婆豆腐都很好。
顾客 :我喜欢吃豆腐，可是不要太辣。
服务员 :没问题。
顾客 :我喜欢吃鱼，给我来一份糖醋鱼。
顾客 :我不喜欢吃肉，来一个汤。
服务员 :要米饭吗？
顾客 :每人来一碗米饭。
服务员 :好的,请稍等，一会儿就好。
顾客 :服务员，再来一瓶啤酒。
服务员 :好的，请稍等。

Waitress:Welcome, have you reservation please?
Customer:Yes, we have.
Waitress:What's your surname please?
Customer:The surname is Li and the full name is Li Ming.
Waitress:Mr Li, you have the Peony room. Please follow me.
Waitress:This is the menu. What do you want to drink?
Customer 1:I would like a bottle of Qingdao beer.
Customer 2:I would like a glass of red wine.
Customer 3:I would like a pot of green tea.
Customer 1:What dishes would you recommend?
Waitress:The spicy chicken pieces and the spicy bean curd are both very good.
Customer 1:I like bean curd, but don't make it too spicy.
Waitress:No problem.
Customer 2:I like eating fish. One sweet-and-sour fish for me please.
Customer 3:I don't like eating meat. One soup please.
Waitress:Any rice?
Customer 1:One bowl of rice for each.
Waitress:OK, please wait a moment. It will be ready soon.
