Making introductions

This section provides you with the key language needed to make contact.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

你好nǐhǎoHello.(informal)
王老师,您好Wáng lǎoshī, nín hǎoHello, Teacher Wang. (formal)
早上好zǎoshàng hǎoGood morning.
晚上好wǎnshàng hǎoGood evening.
好久不见hǎo jiǔ bù jiànLong time no see!
再见zàijiànGoodbye.
明天见míngtiān jiànSee you tomorrow!
下次再聊xiàcì zài liáoTalk with you next time!
你叫什么名字?nǐ jiào shénme míngzìWhat is your name? (informal)
你姓什么?nǐ xìng shénme?What is your surname?
我姓毛,叫毛小龙。wǒ xìng Máo, jiào Máo XiǎolóngMy surname is Mao, I am Xiaolong Mao.
您贵姓?nín guì xìngWhat is your surname? (formal)
免贵姓郁miǎn guì xìng YùMy surname is Yu.
欢迎huānyíngWelcome.
很高兴见到你hěn gāoxìng jiàndào nǐNice to meet you.
喂wèiHello (on the telephone)
我来介绍一下wǒ lái jièshào yīxiàLet me introduce ...

陈经理：你们好，对不起，让你们久等了。
王先生：没关系，我们刚到。陈经理，请允许我们先自我介绍一下，我姓王，这次代表我们公司负责这项计划。这是我的助手小张。
陈经理：很高兴认识你，王先生。很高兴认识你，张小姐。
张小姐：您好，陈经理，很荣幸能与您合作。
陈经理：我也很荣幸，这是我的名片，我们去会议室慢慢谈。


Chén jīnglǐ:Nǐmen hǎo, duìbuqǐ, ràng nǐmen jiǔ děng le.
Wáng xiānsheng:Méi guānxi, wǒmen gāng dào. Chén jīnglǐ, qǐng yúnxǔ wǒmen xiān zìwǒ jièshào yīxià, wǒ xìng Wáng, zhè cì dàibiǎo wǒmen gōngsī fùzé zhè xiàng jìhuà. Zhè shì wǒ de zhùshǒu xiǎo Zhāng.
Chén jīnglǐ:Hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ, Wáng xiānsheng. Hěn gǎoxìng rènshi nǐ, Zhāng xiǎojiě.
Zhāng xiǎojiě:Nín hǎo, Chén jīnglǐ, hěn róngxìng néng yǔ nín hézuò.
Chén jīnglǐ:Wǒ yě hěn róngxìng, zhè shì wǒ de míngpiàn, wǒmen qù huìyìshì mànmàn tán.

Manager Chen:Hello, sorry for keeping you waiting so long.
Mr Wang:No problem, we just arrived. Manager Chen, please allow us to first introduce ourselves. My surname is Wang, and I am currently in charge of the project on behalf of our company. This is my assistant, Junior Zhang.
Manager Chen:Nice to meet you, Mr Wang. Nice to meet you, Miss Zhang.
Miss Zhang:Hello, Manager Chen, it is great honour to cooperate with you.
Manager Chen:My pleasure too, this is my business card, let's go to the meeting room to chat.
