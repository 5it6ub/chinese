Making introductions

This section provides you with the key language needed to make contact.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

你好nǐhǎoHello.(informal)
王老师,您好Wáng lǎoshī, nín hǎoHello, Teacher Wang. (formal)
早上好zǎoshàng hǎoGood morning.
晚上好wǎnshàng hǎoGood evening.
好久不见hǎo jiǔ bù jiànLong time no see!
再见zàijiànGoodbye.
明天见míngtiān jiànSee you tomorrow!
下次再聊xiàcì zài liáoTalk with you next time!
你叫什么名字?nǐ jiào shénme míngzìWhat is your name? (informal)
你姓什么?nǐ xìng shénme?What is your surname?
我姓毛,叫毛小龙。wǒ xìng Máo, jiào Máo XiǎolóngMy surname is Mao, I am Xiaolong Mao.
您贵姓?nín guì xìngWhat is your surname? (formal)
免贵姓郁miǎn guì xìng YùMy surname is Yu.

李森:你好。 我是李森, 你叫什么?
刘明:你好, 李森, 我姓刘, 我叫刘明。
李森:刘明, 你是学生吗?
刘明:对, 我是学生, 我学数学。你呢?
李森:我学管理。你从哪里来?
刘明:我从北京来。你是中国人吗?
李森:不是,我是新加坡人。


Lǐ Sēn:Nǐhǎo. Wǒ shì Lǐ Sēn, nǐ jiào shénme?
Liú Míng:Nǐhǎo, Lǐ Sēn, wǒ xìng Liú, wǒ jiào Liú Míng.
Lǐ Sēn:Liú Míng, nǐ shì xuéshēng ma?
Liú Míng:Duì, wǒ shì xuéshēng, wǒ xué shùxué. Nǐ ne?
Lǐ Sēn:Wǒ xué guǎnlǐ. Nǐ cóng nǎlǐ lái?
Liú Míng:Wǒ cóng Běijīng lái. Nǐ shì Zhōngguórén ma?
Lǐ Sēn:Bù shì, wǒ shì Xīnjiāpōrén.

Sen Li:Hello. I am Sen Li. What is your name?
Ming Liu:Hello, Sen Li. My surname is Liu, I am called Ming Liu.
Sen Li:Ming Liu, are you a student?
Ming Liu:Yes, I am a student. I study Mathematics. And you?
Sen Li:I study Management. Where are you from?
Ming Liu:I am from Beijing. Are you Chinese?
Sen Li:No, I am not. I am Singaporean.
