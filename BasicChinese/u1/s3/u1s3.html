Making introductions

This section provides you with the key language needed to make contact.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

你好nǐhǎoHello.(informal)
王老师,您好Wáng lǎoshī, nín hǎoHello, Teacher Wang. (formal)
早上好zǎoshàng hǎoGood morning.
晚上好wǎnshàng hǎoGood evening.
好久不见hǎo jiǔ bù jiànLong time no see!
再见zàijiànGoodbye.
明天见míngtiān jiànSee you tomorrow!
下次再聊xiàcì zài liáoTalk with you next time!

王 先生:请问您贵姓?
李 小姐:免贵姓李。您呢?
王 先生:我姓王. 李小姐, 您好,很高兴认识您。
李 小姐:王先生, 您好, 我也很高兴认识您。这是我的名片。


Wáng xiānsheng: Qǐngwèn nín guì xìng?
Lǐ xiǎojiě: Miǎn guì xìng Lǐ. Nín ne?
Wáng xiānsheng: Wǒ xìng Wáng. Lǐ xiǎojiě, nín hǎo, hěn gāoxìng rènshi nín.
Lǐ xiǎojiě: Wáng xiānsheng, nín hǎo, wǒ yě hěn gāoxìng rènshi nín. Zhè shì wǒ de míngpiàn.

Mr Wang:Excuse me, what is your surname?
Miss Li:My surname is Li. And yours?
Mr Wang:My surname is Wang. Miss Li, how do you do? I am delighted to meet you.
Miss Li:Mr Wang, how do you do? I am also very happy to meet you. Here is my visiting card.
