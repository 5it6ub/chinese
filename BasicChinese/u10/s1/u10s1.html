In the taxi

Asking for directions

This section provides you with the key language needed for local travel.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

左拐zuǒ guǎiturn left
右拐yòu guǎiturn right
就停在这里吧jiù tíng zài zhèlǐ baJust stop here.
一直往前yīzhí wǎng qiánGo straight ahead.
我想去颐和园wǒ xiǎng qù YíhéyuánI want to go to the Summer Palace.
到了dào leWe've arrived.
第二个路口dì èr gè lùkǒuthe second cross road
上车请投币shàngchē qǐng tóubìPlease toss your coin when getting on the bus.
还有几站？háiyǒu jǐ zhàn?How many more stops?
没问题méi wèntíNo problem.
然后呢？ránhòu ne?And then?
先一直走，然后左拐xiān yīzhí zǒu, ránhòu zuǒguǎiFirst go straight ahead and then turn left.
你知道该怎么走吗？nǐ zhīdào gāi zěnme zǒu ma?Do you know how to get there?
请问去图书馆怎么走？Qǐngwèn qù túshūguǎn zěnme zǒu? May I ask how to get to the library?
请问附近有洗手间吗？Qǐngwèn fùjìn yǒu xǐshǒujiān ma? May I ask if there is a toilet nearby?

Vocabulary for taking a taxi

想xiǎngto want

到dàoto arrive at, at, to
了le[aspect marker]
多少duōshǎohow much, how many
钱qiánmoney
块kuài(colloquial for) Yuan

可以kěyǐcan
收据shōujùreceipt
找zhǎoto give change
师傅shīfuform of address (note)

司机 ：你好, 你去哪里?
乘客 ：你好, 我们想去颐和园。
司机 ：好的。
司机 ：你好。颐和园到了。
乘客 ：好的。多少钱?
司机 ：十七块钱。
乘客 ：给您二十。
司机 ：我找您三块
乘客 ：师傅, 可以给我们收据吗?
司机 ：可以。
乘客 ：谢谢。
  
Sījī:Nǐhǎo, nǐ qù nǎlǐ?
Chéngkè: Nǐhǎo, wǒmen xiǎng qù Yíhéyuán.
Sījī:Hǎo de.
Sījī:Nǐhǎo. Yíhéyuán dào le.
Chéngkè:Hǎo de. Duōshǎo qián?
Sījī:Shí qī kuài qián.
Chéngkè:Gěi nín èr shí.
Sījī:Wǒ zhǎo nín sān kuài.
Chéngkè:Shīfu, kěyǐ gěi wǒmen shōujù ma?
Sījī:Kěyǐ.
Chéngkè:Xièxie.

Driver:Hello, where are you going?
Passenger: Hello, we want to go to the Summer Palace.
Driver:Sure.
Driver:Hi. We have arrived at the Summer Palace.
Passenger:Good. How much is it?
Driver:Seventeen Yuan.
Passenger:Here is twenty.
Driver:Here is your three yuan change.
Passenger:Sir, could you please give us a receipt?
Driver:Yes.
Passenger:Thanks.
