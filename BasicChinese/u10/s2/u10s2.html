Asking for directions

This section provides you with the key language needed for local travel.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

右拐yòu guǎiturn right
就停在这里吧jiù tíng zài zhèlǐ baJust stop here.
一直往前yīzhí wǎng qiánGo straight ahead.
我想去颐和园wǒ xiǎng qù YíhéyuánI want to go to the Summer Palace.
到了dào leWe've arrived.

然后呢？ránhòu ne?And then?

你知道该怎么走吗？nǐ zhīdào gāi zěnme zǒu ma?Do you know how to get there?
请问去图书馆怎么走？Qǐngwèn qù túshūguǎn zěnme zǒu? May I ask how to get to the library?
请问附近有洗手间吗？Qǐngwèn fùjìn yǒu xǐshǒujiān ma? May I ask if there is a toilet nearby?

录音 ：乘客您好，上车请投币。
乘客 ：司机先生，请问多少钱？
司机 ：两元。
乘客 ：好。
乘客 ：对不起，请问我去这个地方应该在哪一站下？
司机 ：你可以在国定路下。
乘客 ：请问还有几站？
司机 ：还有三站。
乘客 ：您到时候能叫我一下吗？
司机 ：好的，没问题。
乘客 ：谢谢您。
  
Lùyīn:Chéngkè nín hǎo, shàngchē qǐng tóubì.
Chéngkè: Sījī xiānsheng, qǐngwèn duōshǎo qián?
Sījī:Liǎng yuán.
Chéngkè:Hǎo.
Chéngkè:Duìbùqǐ, qǐngwèn wǒ qù zhègè dìfang yīnggāi zài nǎ yī zhàn xià?
Sījī:Nǐ kěyǐ zài Guódìng Lù xià.
Chéngkè:Qǐngwèn háiyǒu jǐ zhàn?
Sījī:Háiyǒu sān zhàn.
Chéngke:Nín dào shíhòu néng jiào wǒ yīxià ma?
Sījī:Hǎode, méi wèntí.
Chéngkè:Xièxie nín.

Recorded voice:Hello passengers, please toss coins when getting on the bus.
Passenger:Driver, may I ask how much the fare is please?
Driver:Two yuan.
Passenger:OK.
Passenger:Excuse me, I want to get to this place, where should I get off?
Driver:You may get off at Guoding Road.
Passenger:May I ask how many stops to go?
Driver:Three stops to go.
Passenger:Could you please call me when we arrive?
Driver:Sure, no problem.
Passenger:Thank you.
