Asking for directions

This section provides you with the key language needed for local travel.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

右拐yòu guǎiturn right

一直往前yīzhí wǎng qiánGo straight ahead.
我想去颐和园wǒ xiǎng qù YíhéyuánI want to go to the Summer Palace.
到了dào leWe've arrived.

上车请投币shàngchē qǐng tóubìPlease toss your coin when getting on the bus.

然后呢？ránhòu ne?And then?
先一直走，然后左拐xiān yīzhí zǒu, ránhòu zuǒguǎiFirst go straight ahead and then turn left.
你知道该怎么走吗？nǐ zhīdào gāi zěnme zǒu ma?Do you know how to get there?
请问去图书馆怎么走？Qǐngwèn qù túshūguǎn zěnme zǒu? May I ask how to get to the library?
请问附近有洗手间吗？Qǐngwèn fùjìn yǒu xǐshǒujiān ma? May I ask if there is a toilet nearby?

司机 ：小姐你好，你去哪里？
乘客 ：你好，我去一个朋友家，这是地址。
司机 ：好的。
司机 ：小姐，你知道前面该怎么走吗？
乘客 ：知道。先一直走，然后左拐。
司机 ：好的，然后呢？
乘客 ：前面第二个路口右拐，然后一直往前开。
司机 ：好。
乘客 ：司机先生，到了，就停在这里吧。
  
Sījī:Xiǎojiě nǐhǎo, nǐ qù nǎlǐ?
Chéngkè: Nǐhǎo, wǒ qù yīgè péngyǒu jiā, zhèshì dìzhǐ.
Sījī:Hǎo de.
Sījī:Xiǎojiě, nǐ zhīdào qiánmiàn gāi zěnme zǒu ma?
Chéngkè:Zhīdào. Xiān yīzhí zǒu, ránhòu zuǒ guǎi.
Sījī:Hǎo de, ránhòu ne?
Chéngkè:Qiánmiàn dì èr gè lùkǒu yòu guǎi, ránhòu yīzhí wǎng qián kāi.
Sījī:Hǎo.
Chéngkè:Sījī xiānsheng, dào le, jiù tíng zài zhèlǐ ba

Driver:Hello miss, where are you going?
Passenger:Hello, I am going to a friend's home. This is the address.
Driver:OK.
Driver:Miss, do you know where to go from here?
Passenger:Yes. First go straight, and then turn left.
Driver:OK, and then?
Passenger:Turn right at the second crossroad, and then go ahead.
Driver:OK.
Passenger:Driver, here we are, please stop just here.
