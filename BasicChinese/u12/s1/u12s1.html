At the chemist

Talking to the doctor

This section provides you with the key language needed to talk about health.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

我发烧了wǒ fāshāo leI have a high fever.
你哪里不舒服？Nǐ nǎlǐ bù shūfu?What's wrong with you?
喉咙发炎hóulóng fāyánto have a sore throat
开药片kāi yàopiànto prescribe some medicine
量体温liáng tǐwēnto measure the body temperature

我有点儿胃疼Wǒ yǒu diǎnr wèiténgI have a stomach ache.

药剂师 ： 您好!
顾客 ：您好! 我有点儿不舒服。
药剂师 ：哪里不舒服?
顾客 ：我有点儿胃疼, 还有点儿腹泻。
药剂师 ：多长时间了?
顾客 ：两天了。
药剂师 ：这是给您的药, 疗效很好。
顾客 ：好的, 我试试。一天吃几次?
药剂师 ：一天三次, 饭后吃
顾客 ：好的, 多少钱?
药剂师 ：十元钱。
顾客 ：好,给您的钱。
药剂师 ：谢谢。祝您早日康复! 再见。
顾客 ：好的, 谢谢。
  
Yàojìshī: Nín hǎo!
Gùkè:Nín hǎo! Wǒ yǒu diǎnr bù shūfu.
Yàojìshī:Nǎlǐ bù shūfu?
Gùkè:Wǒ yǒu diǎnr wèiténg, háiyǒu diǎnr fùxiè.
Yàojìshī:Duō cháng shíjiān le?
Gùkè:Liǎng tiān le.
Yàojìshī:Zhè shì gěi nín de yào, liáoxiào hěn hǎo.
Gùkè:Hǎo de, wǒ shìshi. Yī tiān chī jǐ cì?
Yàojìshī:Yī tiān sān cì, fàn hòu chī.
Gùkè:Hǎo de, duōshǎo qián?
Yàojìshī:Shí yuán qián.
Gùkè:Hǎo, gěi nín de qián.
Yàojìshī:Xièxie. Zhù nín zǎorì kāngfù! Zàijiàn.
Gùkè:Hǎo de, xièxie.

Pharmacist: Hello!
Customer:Hello! I feel a bit unwell.
Pharmacist:What's wrong?
Customer:I have a bit of a stomach ache, and diarrhea as well.
Pharmacist:For how long have you had that?
Customer:Two days.
Pharmacist:This is the medicine for you. It's quite effective.
Customer:Fine, I'll have a try. How many times a day?
Pharmacist:Three times a day after meals.
Customer:OK, how much is it?
Pharmacist:Ten yuan.
Customer:Fine, here you are.
Pharmacist:Thanks. Wish you a quick recovery.
Customer:Sure, thanks.
