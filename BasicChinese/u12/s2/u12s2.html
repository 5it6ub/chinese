At the chemist

Talking to the doctor

This section provides you with the key language needed to talk about health.
The key language is relevant to the whole unit.

我发烧了wǒ fāshāo leI have a high fever.
你哪里不舒服？Nǐ nǎlǐ bù shūfu?What's wrong with you?

开药片kāi yàopiànto prescribe some medicine
量体温liáng tǐwēnto measure the body temperature
对青霉素过敏duì qīngméisù guòmǐnbe allergic to penicillin
平时要多喝水píngshí yào duō hēshuǐtake much water at usual time
我有点儿胃疼Wǒ yǒu diǎnr wèiténgI have a stomach ache.
祝您早日康复zhùnín zǎorì kāngfùwish you a quick recovery
一天三次，一次两片yi tiān sān cì, yī cì liǎng piànthree times a day, two tablets each time
饭后吃fàn hòu chītake after meals
我试试wǒ shìshiI'll have a try.
多长时间了？duō cháng shíjiān le?How long has it lasted?

医生 ： 你好，你哪里不舒服？
病人 ：医生，我喉咙疼，而且好像发烧了。
医生 ：把嘴张开，让我看看，说“啊”。
病人 ：啊 ...
医生 ：你喉咙发炎了，我给你开点消炎药片。你发烧几天了？
病人 ：昨天开始发烧的，我自己量过体温，三十八度。
医生 ：我再给你开点退烧片。你对青霉素过敏吗？
病人 ：不过敏。
医生 ：平时注意多喝水，多休息。
病人 ：好的，我知道了，谢谢医生。
医生 ：不客气。
  
Yīsheng: Nǐ hǎo, nǐ nǎlǐ bù shūfu?
Bìngrén: Yīsheng, wǒ hóulong téng, érqiě hǎoxiàng fāshāo le.
Yīsheng:Bǎ zuǐ zhāngkāi, ràng wǒ kànkan, shuō 'ā'.
Bìngrén:Ā ...
Yīsheng:Nǐ hóulong fāyán le, wǒ gěi nǐ kāi diǎn xiāoyán yàopiàn. Nǐ fāshāo jǐ tiān le?
Bìngrén:Zuótiān kāishǐ fāshāo de, wǒ zìjǐ liáng guò tǐwēn, sān shí bā dù.
Yīsheng:Wǒ zài gěi nǐ kāi diǎn tuìshāo piàn. Nǐ duì qīngméisù guòmǐn ma?
Bìngrén:Bù guòmǐn.
Yīsheng:Píngshí zhùyì duō hēshuǐ, duō xīuxi.
Bìngrén:Hǎo de, wǒ zhīdào le, xièxie yīsheng.
Yīsheng:Bù kèqi.

Doctor: Hello, what's wrong with you?
Patient: Doctor, my throat pains, and seemingly I have a high fever.
Doctor:Open the mouth, let me have a look, say 'ah'.
Patient:Ah ...
Doctor:You have a sore throat, I'll prescribe you some anti-infection tablets. For how many days have you had the high fever?
Patient:From yesterday. I've take then temperature myself, 38 degrees.
Doctor:I'll prescribe you some tablet to lower down the temperature. Are you allergic to penicillin?
Patient:No.
Doctor:Do take much water and rest at usual time.
Patient:OK, I know, thank you, doctor.
Doctor:You are welcome.
