Components

Chinese characters are not simply made up of a number of individual strokes. More complex characters can usually be broken down into a number of common constituents which are arranged in a number of regular patterns. Such constitutens are often themselves made up of further small constituents.
Example:
Left/right: 打 Top/bottom: 学 Enclosure: 回, 同, 历, 司, 过
One of the components normaly provides an indication of the meaning of the character as a whole. This part of the character is known as its radical. Recognizing the radical can help in several ways:
  • it can provide a clue to the character's meaning,
  • the non-radical part can provide a clue to the pronunciation of the character,
  • the radicals provide a common way of looking up characters whose pronunciation is not known.
Semantic component
Below are some of the most common semantic components. Note that often, but not always, there is an obvious link between the meaning of the semantic component and the meaning of the character as a whole. Also note, that the pronunciation of the characters varies across characters with the same semantic component.

人, 亻(rén, person),  手, 扌(shǒu, hand),  木(mù, tree),  水氵(shuǐ, water)

心, 忄 (xīn, heart),  火, 灬(huǒ, fire),  食, 饣(shí, food),  车(chē, vehicle),  衣, 衤(yī, clothes),  金, 钅(jīn, gold) (the second form is the radical only variant of the independent character)

人, 亻 : 化 (huà, change), 件 (jiàn, item), 住 (zhù, live), 任 (rén, to appoint)
手,扌 : 打 (dǎ, to hit), 扫 (sǎo, to sweep), 把 (bǎ, handle), 指 (zhǐ, to point)
木 : 机 (jī, machine), 香 (xiāng, fragrant), 林 (lín, forrest), 果 (guǒ, fruit)
水氵 : 江 (jiāng, river), 湖 (hú, lake), 注 (zhù, to pour), 酒 (jiǔ, alcohol)
心, 忄 : 态 (tài, attitude), 忙 (máng, busy), 怕 (pà, fear), 恨 (hèn, to hate)
火, 灬 : 烟 (yān, smoke), 灯 (dēng, lamp), 点 (diǎn, dot), 熟 (shú, cooked)
食, 饣 : 馆 (guǎn, building), 饭 (fàn, food), 饿 (è, hungry), 铰 (jiǎo, dumpling)
车 : 辆 (liàng, MW for cars), 转 (zhuǎn, to turn), 军 (jūn, army), 轮 (lún, wheel)
衣, 衤 : 裤 (kù, ,trousers), 群 (qún, skirt), 衬 (chèn, shirt), 衫 (shirt)
金, 钅 : 刚 (gāng, steel), 钟 (zhōng, bell), 银 (yín, silver), 铜 (tóng, copper)
