Time

The time of day is given in the following way: hour + 点 + minutes + 分
Example:
10:00 十点 (shí diǎn)
10:20 十点二十分 (shí diǎn èrshí fēn)
If the minutes are less then ten, 零 needs to be inserted:
10:05 十点零五分 (shí diǎn líng wǔ fēn)
Times can be given using the 24 hour clock or by explicitly indicating the part of day:
11:00 十一点 or 上午十一点 (shíyī diǎn, shàngwǔ shíyī diǎn)
14:00 十四点 or 下午二点 (shísì diǎn, xiàwǔ èr diǎn)
早上 (zǎoshang) morning, before nine 上午 (shàngwǔ) late morning
中午 (zhōngwǔ) noon  
下午 (xiàwǔ) afternoon 晚上 (wǎnshang) evening
For thirty minutes you can use 半, for quarter hours you can use 刻:
8:30 八点半 (bā diǎn bàn)
16:15 下午四点一刻 (xiàwǔ sì diǎn yī kè)
9:45 九点三刻 (jiǔ diǎn sān kè)
Minutes etc. before the hour are given with 差:
9:50 十点差十分 (shí diǎn chà shí fen) [lit. ten o'clock missing ten minutes]
3:45 四点差一刻 (sì diǎn chà yī kè) [lit. four o'clock missing quarter of an hour]
For seconds use 秒 (miǎo)
