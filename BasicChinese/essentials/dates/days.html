Days

The weekdays are formed by adding the number of the day starting with Monday to 星期 (xīngqī) or 礼拜 (lǐbài), both meaning week.
Monday: 星期一 (xīngqīyī)
Tuesday: 星期二 (xīngqī'er)
Wednesday: 星期三 (xīngqīsān)
Thursday: 星期四 (xingqīsì)
Friday: 星期五 (xīngqīwǔ)
Saturday: 星期六 (xīngqīliù)
Sunday: 星期天 or 星期日 (xīngqīrì or xingqītiān)
The weekend is called: 周末 (zhōumò)
Today is: 今天 (jīntiān)
Tomorrow is: 明天 (míngtiān)
Yesterday is: 昨天 (zuótiān)
Last week is: 上星期 (shàngxīngqī)
Next week is: 下星期 (xiàxīngqī)
