Months

The months are formed by giving the number of the month followed by 月 (yuè)
一月 (yīyuè) January
二月 (èryuè) February
三月 (sānyuè) March
四月 (sìyuè) April
五月 (wǔyuè) May
六月 (liùyuè) June
七月 (qīyuè) July
八月 (bāyuè) August
九月 (jiǔyuè) September
十月 (shíyuè) October
十一月 (shíyīyuè) November
十二月 (shíèryuè) December
Last month is 上个月 (shànggèyuè) Next months is: 下个月 (xiàgèyuè)
