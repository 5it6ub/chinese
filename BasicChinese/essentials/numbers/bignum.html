Big numbers (100-100 000 000)

The major divisions in the Chinese number systems are:
100 百 bǎi
1,000 千 qiān
10,000 万 wàn
100,000,000 亿 yì

Numbers above 10,000 are formed by using wàn:
23,456 二万三千四百五十六 (èr wàn sān qián sì bǎi wǔ shí liù)

All units must be present, insert 零 if needed, e.g.
23,006 二万三千零六 (èr wàn sān qiān líng liù)

A million is 一百万 (lit. one hundred ten thousands)
A billion is 一十亿 (lit. ten times a hundred million)
