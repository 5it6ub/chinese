Quoting numbers

When quoting a number the number is usually given as a series of individual digits.

你的房间号是二三五。 (Nǐ de fángjiān hào shì èr sān wǔ.) Your room number is 235.
今年二〇〇八。 (Jīnnián èr líng líng bā.) This is the year 2008.

When quoting the number 一 is often replaced with 幺 (yāo) for clarity's sake.
