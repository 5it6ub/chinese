Fractions and percentages

Fractions and percentages are formed with ...分之... . The denominator preceeds 分之 the numerator follows it.

Examples:
one third: 三分之一 sān fēnzhī yī
two thirds: 三分之二 sān fēnzhī èr

A percentage is expressed by using 百分之 followed by the amount.

Examples:
30%: 百分之三十 bǎi fēnzhī sānshí
85%: 百分之八十五 bǎi fēnzhī bāshíwǔ

Decimal fractions are formed using 点 (diǎn).

Examples:
1.5: 一点五 yī diǎn wǔ
1.05: 一点零五 yī diǎn líng wǔ
