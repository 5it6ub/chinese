How Many?


To ask 'how many' use 几 (jǐ)
for numbers below 10		 use 多少 (duōshǎo)
for numbers above ten

你家有几口人? (Nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rén?) How many people are in your family?

你有多少钱? (Nǐ yǒu duōshǎo qián?) How much money do you have?
