Countries

Index
Country names are either formed by adding 国 (guó)to a syllable sounding similar to the country's actual name, or the Chinese name is a transcription of the country's name:

中国 zhōngguó China
英国 yīngguó England, Britain
法国 fǎguó France
德国 déguó Germany
美国 měiguó United States
俄国 éguó Russia
韩国 hánguó Korea

瑞典 ruìdiǎn Schweden
瑞士 ruìshì Switzerland
越南 yuènán Vietnam

To ask what country somebody is from, say:
你是哪国人? (nǐ shì nǎ guó rén) What country are you from?
